About Us

For everyday since 2005, SA Coro Pack is making the best packaging boxes. We help many types of businesses like FMCG, Pharma, Cosmetics, Cloth shops, Retail shops, and Online sellers.

We have many products like Corrugated Boxes, Fancy Boxes, Printed Boxes, Ice Cream Boxes, Pizza Boxes, Chocolate Boxes, and more. We make boxes exactly how your business needs them. Whether you need a small box or a big heavy-duty box, we can make it for you with your own design.

Best Quality & Eco-Friendly

We promise to give you very good quality products. Our boxes are strong, have a perfect finish, and last for a long time. They are waterproof, heat-resistant, and keep your items safe. You can also customize them as per your wish.

We also care about nature. That is why we provide sustainable and eco-friendly packaging solutions that are good for the environment and also easy on your pocket.

Our Vision & Mission

Driving reliable, sustainable packaging that strengthens supply chains and elevates brands.

Vision Statement

To be the leading provider of innovative, sustainable, and high-quality packaging solutions by leveraging advanced automation, fostering a culture of continuous improvement, and prioritizing customer satisfaction.

  • Champion eco-friendly materials and circular design
  • Reduce breakage and returns through robust engineering
  • Elevate brand experiences with premium prints and finishes
  • Expand reliable supply across regions with consistent quality
  • Continuously innovate for lighter, stronger, cost-efficient packaging

Our Aspiration

Our goal is to make every box a testament to quality, creativity, and care. We envision a future where packaging not only protects products but also tells a story of brand values and commitment to sustainability.

Through collaborative relationships with our customers, suppliers, and community, we aspire to drive positive change and set standards for the packaging industry.

  • Collaborate to map SKUs, volumes and packaging goals
  • Engineer board grades, flute profiles and sizing for optimal protection
  • Run print-ready proofs and quick sampling for approvals
  • Maintain quality gates: ECT/Burst tests, adhesion, moisture checks
  • Ensure on-time production, tracked dispatches and after-sales support
  • Adopt sustainable inputs and compliance with industry standards
